DR. Rilwan Basaru

ABOUT ME

Dr. Rilwan is a Senior Machine Learning Engineer with a PhD in Computer Vision and over 8 years of experience developing and deploying machine learning systems in real-time, high-performance environments. He has led the development of ML models across autonomous driving, industrial robotics, and medical diagnostics, with a strong emphasis on edge deployment, scalable infrastructure, and safety-critical applications. Proficient in Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, CUDA, and cloud technologies (AWS/GCP), Dr. Basaru combines deep technical expertise with a collaborative, outcome-driven approach to ML system design and deployment.
